FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate is the paycheck calculator?
Our calculator uses current 2025 federal tax brackets and state tax rates. It accounts for standard deductions, FICA (Social Security and Medicare), and common filing statuses. Results are estimates — your actual paycheck may vary based on pre-tax deductions like 401(k) contributions, health insurance premiums, or local taxes not captured here.
Is my salary information saved anywhere?
No. All calculations happen entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to our servers, stored, or shared with anyone. Your financial information is completely private.
Can I use the pay stub generator for loan applications?
Our pay stub generator creates professional formatted documents for record-keeping and informational purposes. Pay stubs should only be created with accurate, truthful information. Creating a pay stub with false income information for the purpose of deceiving a lender is fraud.
How often is the minimum wage data updated?
We review and update state minimum wage data when new rates take effect, typically January 1st or July 1st each year. We note scheduled future increases so you can plan ahead. Always verify with your state's Department of Labor for the most current official rate.
